Functionality for capturing ASL hand images
run main.py in this directory
Your webcam should turn on and a window should appear

click 'c' to capture an image
- the image will be saved at ./images/captured_image1.jpg
- if you click 'c' again it will be saved at ./images/captured_image2.jpg ...etc.
- if you rerun and ./images/captured_image1.jpg already exist, it will be overwritten

click 'd' to delete all images in ./images/

click 'q' to quit, close window and turn off webcam
